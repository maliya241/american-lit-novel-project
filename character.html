Main Characters Map

Summary

  • Dr. David Henry (McCallister) – He is an orthopedic surgeon who sends his newborn daughter who has Down’s syndrome away and says she died.
  • Norah Asher – She is married to David Henry and is the mother of Paul and Phoebe.
  • Paul Henry – He is born first and healthy. He is the older twin brother of Phoebe.
  • Phoebe Henry – She is born second and has Down’s syndrome. She is sent away from her family by her father and lives with Caroline Gill. She is Paul’s twin.
  • Caroline Gill – She is the nurse who works with David Henry and raises Phoebe.
  • June McCallister – She is David’s sister who had a heart defect and died.
  • Brigitte “Bree” Asher – She is Norah’s sister who is a free spirit.
  • Albert “Al” Simpson – He is a truck driver who helps Caroline when she first has Phoebe under her care. He takes an interest in her.
  • Dorothy “Doro” March – She hires Caroline to take care of her father Leo March.
  • Leo March – He is the father of Doro and needs a caretaker because he is senile.
  • Rosemary – She is a pregnant runaway teen who is found living in David’s abandoned childhood home.
